Recent Projects

Project 1

Eco track

A web application designed to help users track and reduce their carbon footprint. Users can log daily activities, such as commuting and energy consumption, to receive personalized insights and suggestions for a more sustainable lifestyle. Built with React, Node.js, and MongoDB, the app integrates with APIs for real-time environmental data.

Project 2

City Spots

A location-based service for discovering local events, restaurants, and hidden gems in your city. Users can explore curated recommendations, read reviews, and create itineraries for their outings. Built with Angular and integrated with Google Maps API, CitySpots enhances urban exploration experiences.

Project 3

Fithive

A fitness and wellness mobile app that provides personalized workout plans, meal tracking, and progress analytics. It uses AI-powered recommendations to create tailored fitness regimens based on user goals and activity levels. Developed using Flutter and Firebase, the app includes social features like group challenges and leaderboards.

Project 4

LearnSphere

An e-learning platform for students and professionals to access bite-sized courses in technology, design, and business. It features interactive quizzes, video lessons, and gamified achievements. The platform uses Django for the backend and Vue.js for the frontend, with AWS handling scalable deployments.

Project 5

Pet Connect

A social network for pet lovers to connect, share tips, and find pet care resources like veterinarians, trainers, and pet-friendly locations. PetConnect includes forums, a marketplace for pet supplies, and an adoption portal. Developed with MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) stack, it prioritizes a user-friendly interface and strong community engagement.
